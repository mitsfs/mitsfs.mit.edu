MITSFS-Related Publishers and Stores

(See some of the general archives at top for general lists of publishers.)
  • The Space-Crime Continuum (Northampton, MA) supplies most of our paperbacks.
  • The New England Mobile Book Fair (Newton Highlands, MA) supplies most of our hardcovers.
  • NESFA Press, the publishing pseudopod of NESFA, is a respected non-profit small press in SF. They send us copies of the books free; aren't you other publishers jealous of them? Don't you wish you did that too?
  • MITSFS President & Skinner '66 Mike Ward publishes e-books at Hidden Knowledge.
  • MITSFS President & Skinner '84 Tim Huckelbery operates the mind-control lasers for the Games Workshop; they do tabletop wargaming (miniatures) and their Black Library arm publishes related fiction.
  • And these stores give discounts to MITSFS members.
